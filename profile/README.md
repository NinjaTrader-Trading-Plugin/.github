## 🚀 Summary
NinjaTrader for Windows is a desktop trading platform that unites charting, market analysis, strategy backtesting, and order execution for futures and forex traders. The platform is best known for how much control it gives you over indicators: you can reshape the built-in NinjaTrader indicators, blend them together, or write custom ones for your approach. An add-ons ecosystem rounds things out, letting you extend the platform as your trading develops.

## ✨ Feature Set
1. **Advanced charting** — Study markets across timeframes with a fast, configurable charting engine. 📊
2. **Customizable indicators** — Adjust the built-in NinjaTrader indicators or code your own to fit your reading of the market. 🎛️
3. **Strategy backtesting** — Evaluate a rule set on historical data before risking real capital. 🧪
4. **Order execution** — Place, modify, and manage futures and forex orders from the chart. 🖱️
5. **Add-ons ecosystem** — Extend the platform with additional indicators and tools from a broad library. 🧩

## ❓ FAQ
**Is NinjaTrader free?**
NinjaTrader is free for advanced charting, backtesting, and simulated trading. Live trading is available through paid options offered as a lease or a lifetime license, and additional data or brokerage fees may apply depending on your connection.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
NinjaTrader supports modern 64-bit Windows versions, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping Windows updated helps the platform run smoothly with many charts and indicators open.

**Is NinjaTrader legit?**
Yes. NinjaTrader is an established, legitimate trading platform and brokerage that has served futures and forex traders for many years. Trading always involves risk, so review the platform's own materials and your risk tolerance before trading live.

**How does NinjaTrader compare to Tradovate?**
Both are futures-focused platforms. NinjaTrader is a desktop application known for customizable indicators, backtesting, and its add-ons ecosystem, while Tradovate centers on a cloud and browser experience. They differ in fee models and tooling, so the right choice depends on your workflow and platform preference.

**Can I build my own indicators?**
Yes. Beyond the popular indicators bundled by default, you can customize them or develop new ones, and you can install extra indicators and add-ons from the ecosystem.
